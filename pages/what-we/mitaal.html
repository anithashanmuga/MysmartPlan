SUBMITTALEZ

Streamline Your Workflow

POWERED BY MYSMARTPLANS

How many hours have you spent searching for an MIA submittal, RFI, change order, or other workflow document? How much time and money has been spent just to ship or hand deliver documents to the right person? In any industry, there is a focus on productivity. We can't afford to waste precious time just hunting down or waiting on documents. Every hour you spend seeking out missing documents is an hour that could have been spent doing anything else.

PROJECT INFORMATION MANAGER

Every account is assigned a Project Information Manager (PIM). Working with you from the very beginning, a PIM is your access to real, live human support that is always a quick phone call or email away! Need to adjust your workflow? Have a question? Your PIM is at the ready!

CUSTOMIZED DASHBOARD

Your PIM will work with you to customize the workflows to the needs of your project or company. Workflows can be used for submittals, change orders, and so much more. All this in a workflow built to your needs, not a cookie cutter flow.

REAL-TIME DOCUMENT EXCHANGE

With SubmittalEZ, you can see every document within the workflow at all times and know who is working on it (or holding it up!). Attach more documents with just a click of the mouse. You will always know the status of your workflow, as well as have the ability to track any revisions.
