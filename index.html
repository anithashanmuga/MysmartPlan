MySmartPlans brings accountability and transparency to construction, improving your peace of mind and bottom line.


The only company of its kind, MySmartPlans gives owners full control over their documentation through the MySmartPlans software and personal Project Information Managers (PIMs).

MySmartPlans serves as a third party custodian of all the project data, to include, Plans, Specs, Review, Weather, Submittals, RFI’s, Emails, Reports, Photos, Transmittals, etc. Files are Audited and Verified, for all stakeholders.Knowledge Management built for turnover, integration into all systems.

From the smallest projects to the largest, MySmartPlans makes your construction process completely transparent, organized, and ready for the lifecycle management of your project. MySmartPlans eliminates misinformation, shoddy data, overruns, or litigation as a result of document mismanagement. Say goodbye to extra costs sneaked into the pipeline when you weren’t looking or because you couldn’t access your own documents.

Eliminate Data Disasters!

Do you find your current construction management software a challenge to locate accurate, audited, verified information? Who do you trust? Who verifies your knowledge on your project? Your project data holds all your knowledge, 80% of every project ends in costly litigation, prepare from day one for protection.

MySmartPlans’ Project Information Mangers (PIMs) efficiently handle the entire stream of documentation for you. With just one point of entry and exit, that means no more mistakes, unauthorized changes, or unapproved deletions. And YOU are the owner of your documents, not a third party. Let us manage the paper so you can get back to managing your projects.

Closeout From Day One (Red Zone Ready)

Every job you do is important. Maintaining documents for each completed project is essential, but a real hassle. MySmartPlans ensures that all transferred data is available to maintenance staff the day construction is completed. With our Smart Rooms, all project owners feel at peace that their staff have the information they’ll need at the touch of a button.

Processes Built For You

The MySmartPlans system handles each and every phase of your projects, from pre-con to construction to closeout. No more sifting through generic templates to find what you want—with MySmartPlans, each project has its own custom-built dashboard specific to that project’s exact needs.

Integration Across The Board

MySmartPlans gathers information from systems such as Kuaha, Procore, Excel, BIM 360, and other industry standards. Once your information is imported, the MySmartPlans team audits, organizes, and archives all data as necessary for your project.


MySmartPlans includes unlimited users, unlimited storage, and unlimited bandwidth. We are file agnostic, which drives accountability and transparency.
